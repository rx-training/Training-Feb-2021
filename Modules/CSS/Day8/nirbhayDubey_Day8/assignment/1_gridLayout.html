Why Yoga?

  • Balance
  • Becoming flexible is not the goal. Standing on your hands is not the goal. The goal is serenity.Balance. Truly finding peace in your own skin.
  • Fitness
  • Fitness is not about being better than someone else. It’s about being better than you used to be.
